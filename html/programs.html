Games









Rock-Paper-Scissors:The Animation:The Game

A game I independently developed in Unity. My goal was to create an over-the-top rendition of Rock Paper Scissors. Graphics and Animations made in Illustrator, Photoshop, After Effects and Aseprite. Music by Dennis S. Mowers.







Pongman

A game created by Jonathan Dalton and Ammar Karim. Play as the young and brave Pongman, destined to defeat the man destroying the city, Chase Backer. Music composed by Jakob Rush.









Blop

A game I designed where you play as a block that hops. The game keeps track of your score and progressively gets harder.
